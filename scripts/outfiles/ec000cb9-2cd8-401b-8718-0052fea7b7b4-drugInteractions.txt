                              Patients receiving narcotics, antihistamines, antipsychotics, antianxiety agents, or other CNS depressants (including alcohol) concomitantly with TussiCapsÂ® extended-release capsules may exhibit an additive CNS depression.                               The use of MAO inhibitors or tricyclic antidepressants with hydrocodone preparations may increase the effect of either the antidepressant or hydrocodone. 
                              The concurrent use of other anticholinergics with hydrocodone may produce paralytic ileus.
