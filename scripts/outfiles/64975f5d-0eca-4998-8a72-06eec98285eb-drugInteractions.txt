
                     
                     
                     Drug Interactions
                     
                        Plasma levels of anticonvulsant agents may become subtherapeutic during cisplatin therapy. 
                        In a randomized trial in advanced ovarian cancer, response duration was adversely affected when pyridoxine was used in combination with altretamine (hexamethylmelamine) and Cisplatin injection.
