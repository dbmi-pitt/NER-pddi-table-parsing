Follow patients closely for signs of respiratory depression and sedation <content stylecode="italics">[<linkhtml href="#LINK_1440eef7-0ab1-4a44-84fa-ed681cde7fe3">Warnings and Precautions (5.4</linkhtml>)]</content>.</paragraph>
